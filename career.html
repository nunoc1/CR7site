Cristiano Ronaldo Career

Clubs

Cristiano Ronaldo has had a wonderful career, winning numerous titles and playing for several great clubs.

He started in Sporting FC, a portuguese popular club, There he displayed good gameplay and stood out, transferring to Man UTD.Man UTD His peak began in England, where he won a Champions League and a Ballon d'Or. And after years he was bought by Real Madrid.

Real Madrid

At the Spanish club, Robozao, as he is known, experienced the peak of his career, winning numerous titles and scoring countless goals, one of the most remarkable periods in football history.

Juventus

The Portuguese player may not have reached his peak, but he had a good spell with the Italian team. Many people believe that was a bad part of his career, but it wasn't; he scored many goals and won titles.

Man UTD

Back in Manchester, even with an extremely limited team, Cristiano made his appearances, and although it was a more discreet spell, the striker managed to stand out.

Al Nassr
